About LNJPIT,Chapra

Loknayak jai prakash Institute Of Technology popularly knows as LNJPIT Chapra was established in the year 2012 by Government of Bihar under Department of Science and Technology.The idea was to cater growing needs of increasing Technical Demands in our modern life.Spreads over an area of about 45 acres on the banks of the river Ganga,the college has become one of the best new engineering colleges in Bihar. Currently,the college offers 4 years of Bachelor in technology programs in 4 different discipline of civil,mechanical,computer science and electrical and electronics engineering with an annual intake of 240 students.All the courses are affiliated to All India Council For Technical Education(AICTE)Delhi.
