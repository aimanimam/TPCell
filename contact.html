Training and placement cell (TPC) of the institute handles all aspects of placements at LNJPIT,Chapra for the graduating students of all departments. Right from contacting companies to managing all logistics of arranging for tests, pre placement talks and conducting final interviews the Training and Placement Cell officials and volunteers provide their best possible assistance to the recruiters.
